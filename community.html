# WireWhat Live Community Guidelines *Host at a public URL and set `COMMUNITY_GUIDELINES_URL` in release builds, or use the in-app screen.* **Last updated:** June 2026 WireWhat Live connects viewers and nearby broadcasters for live help. Use the app lawfully, safely, and with respect for others. ## Do - Use WireWhat Live only for **lawful** purposes. - Request or broadcast only in places you are **allowed** to observe or record. - Respect **privacy** — avoid filming people, homes, or private property without permission. - Be **respectful** in chat and on camera. - Use **Report** during a live session for safety, privacy, or abuse concerns. ## Don't - Harass, threaten, or intimidate others. - Share sensitive personal information (addresses, ID documents, financial details). - Stream illegal activity, violence, or explicit content. - Impersonate others or misrepresent your location or intent. - Spam notifications or abuse the service. ## Safety WireWhat Live does **not** verify users. You are responsible for your own safety. If you feel unsafe, leave the stream and report the issue. ## Enforcement We may remove access, delete accounts, or report serious violations to authorities. See the privacy policy for how to close your account or contact support.
