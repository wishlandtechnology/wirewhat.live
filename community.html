WireWhat Live Community Guidelines

Last Updated: June 2026

WireWhat Live connects viewers and nearby broadcasters for live help. Use the app lawfully, safely, and with respect for others.

Do

  • Use WireWhat Live only for lawful purposes.
  • Request or broadcast only in places you are allowed to observe or record.
  • Respect privacy and avoid filming people, homes, or private property without permission.
  • Be respectful in chat and on camera.
  • Use the Report feature during a live session for safety, privacy, or abuse concerns.

Don't

  • Harass, threaten, or intimidate others.
  • Share sensitive personal information such as addresses, ID documents, or financial details.
  • Stream illegal activity, violence, or explicit content.
  • Impersonate others or misrepresent your location or intent.
  • Spam notifications or abuse the service.

Safety

WireWhat Live does not verify users. You are responsible for your own safety and decisions. If you feel unsafe, leave the stream and report the issue immediately.

Enforcement

We may remove content, suspend access, delete accounts, or report serious violations to relevant authorities when required by law.

Please refer to our Privacy Policy for information about account deletion requests and data handling practices.
