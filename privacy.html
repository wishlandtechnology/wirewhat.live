WireWhat Live Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how WireWhat Live, a product of Wishland Technology Private Limited, collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our services.

Data We Collect and Why

Phone Number

Used for one-time verification (OTP) when you sign in. We do not sell your phone number or share it for marketing purposes.

Name and General Location

Your name and general location (city or area entered during registration) are used to identify you in chat and help match viewers with nearby publishers.

Precise Location (GPS)

Used to match viewers with nearby publishers, announce live streams, and attach approximate location information to uploaded replays. Location data may be sent to our servers when you use location-based features such as requesting a stream, going live, uploading a replay, or remaining available as a viewer or publisher.

Camera and Microphone

Used when you broadcast live or record a video within the app. Live video and audio are streamed through our infrastructure and delivered to viewers who join the session. Recorded videos may be processed on our servers and may be published to WireWhat Live's official YouTube channel for replay viewing.

Optional Stream Descriptions and Chat Messages

Used to help viewers and publishers communicate and coordinate during live sessions.

Device Token (Push Notifications)

Used to notify publishers about incoming requests and viewers about nearby live streams and service updates.

Live Streams vs Recorded Replays

Live Streams

Live streams are delivered in real time. We do not permanently store live video on our servers after a session ends unless you explicitly choose to save the stream for replay publishing.

Recorded Replays

Videos uploaded for replay may be stored temporarily for processing, including watermarking and publishing. Replays may be published to our official YouTube channel and made available to users. Titles, descriptions, and approximate location information may be shown to nearby viewers searching for replays.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Verify user identity and account ownership.
  • Match viewers and publishers.
  • Deliver live video, audio, and chat services.
  • Send push notifications.
  • Process and publish optional video replays.
  • Improve platform performance, reliability, and security.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Information may be shared only when necessary to operate the service, including cloud hosting providers, SMS providers for OTP verification, mapping services, YouTube replay publishing, analytics tools, or when required by law.

Location data is collected only to enable location-based features within the service and is not used for advertising or sold to third parties.

Your Choices

  • You may decline location, camera, or microphone permissions, although some features may not function properly.
  • You may sign out of the application at any time.
  • You may request deletion of your account or personal data by contacting us.

Account and Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data by contacting us at the email address below. We will make reasonable efforts to process deletion requests within a reasonable period, subject to legal and operational requirements.

Safety

WireWhat Live does not independently verify all users. Please exercise caution when interacting with others. Do not share sensitive personal information through chat, live streams, or recorded content. Respect the privacy and rights of others at all times.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this page and through links provided in the application and app store listings.
